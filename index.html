Veritas Margin Builder is a deal registration program that allows you to register your qualifying new license Backup Exec, Desktop and Laptop Option (DLO) and System Recovery deals.
 
 
 
The registration process takes less than two minutes to complete, making it quick and easy to register.

Principal Member of the Mid-Market Backup and Recovery competency

Partners who are not members of the Mid-Market Backup and Recovery competency
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
*Partner must be a member of Veritas Partner Force Program.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
How it works:
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
STEP 1:
Identify a New License Opportunity
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Click here for eligible Veritas products that can be registered in the Margin Builder Program. Register your deal using the Margin Builder tool and select the Authorized Distributor you will place the order through.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
STEP 2:
Registering a Deal and Deal Validation
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
If your deal meets all of the eligibility requirements of the Margin Builder benefit, Veritas will approve your Margin Builder registration and issue a unique Margin Builder ID number. You and your selected distributor will be notified of approval by email.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
STEP 3:
Claim your Additional Discount
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Provide the Margin Builder ID number to your selected Authorized Distributor to receive your discount.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
For more information on Veritas Margin Builder, please contact your Distributors or email: APJhelpdesk@marginbuilder.com
